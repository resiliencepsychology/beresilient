Resilience Psychology

We are a group of registered Psychologists who are dedicated to improving your emotional wellbeing. We bring all of our experience and training to meet your needs.

Located in Central and West Auckland

We provide treatment and support for a wide range of issues, including:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Stress
  • Phobias
  • Workplace challenges
  • Behavioural change
  • Life's ups and downs
  • Chronic Pain
  • Adjusting to serious illness or loss
  • Managing chronic health conditions
  • Panic attacks
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  • Post-traumatic stress
  • Social anxiety
  • Eating problems
